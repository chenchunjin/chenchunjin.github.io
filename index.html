    Chunjin Chen (陈春锦)¶

    Curriculum Vitae

    Email: chenchunjin@mail.ustc.edu.cn

    Research Interest: My research interest lies in sustainable development and environmental management, integrating both natural and social sciences. I am particularly focused on investigating the effects of climate change and assessing the efficiency of environmental policies and governance.

    I employ causal inference methods such as the difference-in-difference (DID) model, regression discontinuity (RDD) model, and linear regression model. Additionally, I am particularly interested in applying machine learning techniques to develop climate models and predict climate change trends, as well as the impact of natural hazards and extreme weather events.

    I have received specialized training in satellite data processing and am currently learning applications of ArcGIS. I am proficient in using Matlab, Python, R, and Stata for data analysis and modeling.
