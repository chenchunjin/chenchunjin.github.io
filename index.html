    chunjin chen (陈春锦)¶

    Curriculum Vitae

    Email: chenchunjin@mail.ustc.edu.cn

    Research Interest: My research interests include lithium isotope, magmatic-hydrothermal evolution, global climate change, etc. I believe that computer programs can help geologists work out complex geological processes. Therefore, I am also interested in geochemical thermodynamics visualization, numerical simulation, geological big data, and machine learning and have tried to do some preliminary work.

    Research Works¶

    My recent works:

    • Highly differentiated granite:Silicic magma reservoirs and pegmatite-type rare-metal mineralization.
    • Continental weathering and climate change:Li Isotope fractionation during the Late Devonian Frasnian–Famennian (F–F) event.
    • GeoTVP:An open source geochemical thermodynamic reaction web platform. You can try some demos of thermodynamic calculations our website.
